FAQs

1. What is Daily Test?

Daily Test is a series of full length tests provided daily, except Sundays, to run students through hard practice every day.

Daily Test Set contains 100 Mixed Questions across all subjects:

  • 25 Questions from English - Covers all chapters
  • 15 Questions from Mathematics - Randomly selected from 30+ Chapters
  • 30 Questions from General Studies
  • 10 Questions from General Knowledge
  • 10 Questions from General Intelligence / Reasoning
  • 10 Questions from Daily Current Affairs

✅There are no limit of attempts for a Daily Test.

✅Daily Test updates every day by 12 PM.

✅Daily Test shows your Score/accuracy/time taken etc.

✅After completing the test, you can view the Wrongly Attempted Questions with correct answer only in One Liner Format to revise it quickly and take a note.

2. What about Chapterwise Tests?

✅There are chapter/topicwise tests available from all subjects for members only. One can either select a subject/chapter/topic and Start Test; or, search for a chapter/topic from the Search Bar directly on the Test Desk.

✅It covers almost all chapters across all subjects. The questions are set in a way that it covers from basic to advanced level. Organised conceptually — so that, it runs in parallel to books but in test forms.

✅There are no limit of attempts.

3. How Current Affairs are provided?

✅Current Affairs are provided in MCQ Test format in 3 ways.

  1. Daily — 10 Daily CA are mixed in the respective daily set
  2. Weekly — 100 CA from last 10 days are provided in the Test Desk as full sets on Sundays. This is available in both English and Bengali languages.
  3. Monthly — 300/310 Questions are updated as a single monthly test in the Test Desk along with its Downloadable PDF format.

✅There are no limit of attempts.

4. What about Membersihp?

✅Membership validity is 1 Year.
