FAQs

WHAT IS MIXED100?

Mixed100 Tests are 100-MCQ tests in lucid English language, featuring 85-90 subject-wise and 10-15 daily Current Affairs questions, ensuring balanced preparation.

WHAT ARE MIXED IN 100?

100 questions covers all subjects; English, History, Geography, Indian Polity, Indian Economy, Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Reasoning, Computer (Basic) and Current Affairs.

MIXED100s ARE CONDUCTED ON?

Daily tests are conducted on six days a week (excluding Sundays). Sundays have Sunday Affairs. New set is updated everyday in the first half, by 12 PM, of the day.

WHAT IS SUNDAY AFFAIRS?

Sundays feature 100 Current Affairs questions, focusing on the past 10 days for revision. Sourced from PIB, Newsonair, and national newspapers, Sunday Affairs is free for everyone.

QUESTIONS ARE TAKEN FROM?

Most questions are from textbooks, renowned references, and WBCS/UPSC-specific books, with many sourced from past central and state exam papers for comprehensive preparation.

QUESTION LEVEL?

Questions range from basic to advanced, covering exams like SSC CGL, CHSL, MTS, Railways, state PSCs, WBCS Prelims & Mains (non-descriptive), and UPSC Prelims.


Mixed100 are set Moderate to Hard

DOES A TEST SHOW SCORE?

Test report displays score, time taken, number of correct attempts, wrong attempts, skipped questions, and accuracy percentage (need all questions be attempted).

WHO CAN TAKE MIXED100 TESTS?

Only members can access Monday to Saturday Mixed100 tests. Non-members can take the daily Special GK Quiz and Sunday Affairs.

CAN I PRINT/SAVE THE TEST SETS AS PDFS?

The Testkeen Test Sets can only be accessed on devices and is not available for download or printing.

DO WE REALLY NEED DAILY PRACTICE?

Daily practice helps identify strengths and weaknesses, promotes improvement, and boosts confidence, ensuring candidates are well-prepared for competitive exams.
