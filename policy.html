Privacy and Data Usage Policy


At Testkeen™, we are committed to providing a secure and enriching experience for users participating in mock tests for competitive exams in Bharat. We prioritize the privacy and confidentiality of our users' information. Below are key points outlining our privacy and data usage policy:


User Data Collection:

  1. We do not collect or save any private credentials for users accessing our free/demo tests.
  2. For paid users, we collect Names, Gmail addresses, and Mobile Numbers solely for subscription validation and access to paid tests.

Data Storage:

  1. We do not store sensitive information such as passwords, date of birth, gender, images, locations, or any personal history related to test sessions, performances, or scores.
  2. The information collected is limited to Names, Gmail addresses, and Mobile Numbers stored securely for the sole purpose of managing subscription validity.

Communication:

  1. Users opting for paid services are added to Testkeen™'s Google Group (Basic) using their Gmail addresses.
  2. This facilitates communication regarding subscription validity and other relevant updates.
  3. No members of the Google Group can see/communicate/copy the credentials of the other members of the same group.

Restrictions on Data Usage:

  1. The data presented in our tests is not intended to be copied, saved, shared, printed, or used for any form of reproduction (by means of electronic or any other).
  2. Users are encouraged to respect the proprietary nature of the content and refrain from unauthorized use or distribution.

Anonymous Testing:

  1. Users participating in free/demo tests can do so anonymously, without the need for providing personal information.

No Sale of Personal Information:

  1. We do not sell or trade any personal information collected from users to third parties.

Secure Payment Processing:

  1. For users making payments, our website uses secure payment processing services to ensure the confidentiality of financial information.

Changes to Privacy and Data Usage Policy:

  1. At Testkeen™, we are committed to keeping our users informed about how their data is handled. While we strive to maintain a consistent privacy and data usage policy, we reserve the right to make changes to the policy without prior notice. Users are encouraged to regularly review this page for any updates.
  2. Any modifications made to the privacy and data usage policy will be effective immediately upon posting on this page. By continuing to use our services after any changes are made, users implicitly agree to the revised policy.
  3. We understand the importance of transparency, and any significant changes will be communicated through prominent notices on our website.

We assure you that our commitment to data privacy is paramount, and we continually strive to create a secure and trustworthy environment for our users. If you have any concerns or questions regarding our privacy and data usage policy, please feel free to contact us at mail.testkeen@gmail.com.
